## Introduction

Welcome to the Credit Cooperative System! This user manual is designed to help you navigate and use the system effectively as a member. The Credit Cooperative System provides a comprehensive platform for managing your savings, loans, and other cooperative services.

## Getting Started

### Registration

To register for an account in the Credit Cooperative System:

1. Visit the login page and click on "Register" or "Sign Up"
2. Fill out the registration form with your personal information:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (must be at least 8 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters)
   - Phone Number
   - Address
3. Read and accept the Data Privacy Agreement
4. Click "Register" to submit your application
5. Check your email for a verification link
6. Click the verification link to activate your account

### Login

To log in to your account:

1. Visit the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Login"
5. If you've forgotten your password, click "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions sent to your email

### Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see your dashboard with the following sections:

- **Welcome Message**: Displays your name and member ID
- **Account Summary**: Shows your total savings balance
- **Loan Statistics**: Displays information about your active loans
- **Recent Transactions**: Shows your most recent transactions
- **Quick Links**: Provides quick access to common actions
- **Damayan Widget**: Shows information about active mutual aid programs

## Account Management

### Viewing Profile Information

To view your profile information:

1. Click on your name or profile picture in the top-right corner
2. Select "Profile" from the dropdown menu
3. Your profile page will display your personal information, including:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Address
   - Member ID
   - Membership Date

### Updating Profile Information

To update your profile information:

1. Navigate to your profile page
2. Click "Edit Profile" or "Update Information"
3. Update the necessary fields
4. Click "Save Changes" to apply the updates

### Changing Password

To change your password:

1. Navigate to your profile page
2. Click "Change Password" or "Security Settings"
3. Enter your current password
4. Enter your new password
5. Confirm your new password
6. Click "Update Password" to save the changes

### Privacy Settings

To manage your privacy settings:

1. Navigate to your profile page
2. Click "Privacy Settings"
3. Adjust your privacy preferences
4. Click "Save Settings" to apply the changes

## Savings Management

### Viewing Savings Accounts

To view your savings accounts:

1. Click "Savings" in the main navigation menu
2. The page will display all your savings accounts with the following information:
   - Account Type
   - Account Number
   - Current Balance
   - Interest Rate
   - Last Transaction Date

### Making Deposits

To make a deposit to your savings account:

1. Navigate to the Savings page
2. Select the account you want to deposit to
3. Click "Deposit" or "Add Funds"
4. Enter the deposit amount
5. Add a description (optional)
6. Select the payment method
7. Click "Submit" to process the deposit

### Making Withdrawals

To make a withdrawal from your savings account:

1. Navigate to the Savings page
2. Select the account you want to withdraw from
3. Click "Withdraw" or "Get Funds"
4. Enter the withdrawal amount
5. Add a description (optional)
6. Select the withdrawal method
7. Click "Submit" to process the withdrawal

### Viewing Interest Earned

To view the interest earned on your savings:

1. Navigate to the Savings page
2. Select the account you want to view
3. Click "Interest History" or "View Details"
4. The page will display a history of interest earned on the account

### Time Deposits

To open a time deposit:

1. Navigate to the Savings page
2. Click "Open Time Deposit" or "New Account"
3. Select the time deposit product
4. Enter the deposit amount
5. Select the term (duration)
6. Review the interest rate and maturity date
7. Click "Submit" to open the time deposit

## Loan Management

### Viewing Available Loan Products

To view available loan products:

1. Click "Loans" in the main navigation menu
2. Select "Loan Products" or "Apply for Loan"
3. The page will display all available loan products with the following information:
   - Loan Type
   - Interest Rate
   - Minimum and Maximum Amounts
   - Minimum and Maximum Terms
   - Processing Fee
   - Requirements

### Applying for a Loan

To apply for a loan:

1. Navigate to the Loans page
2. Click "Apply for Loan" or select a specific loan product
3. Follow the multi-step application process:
   - Step 1: Select the loan type
   - Step 2: Enter loan details (amount, term, purpose)
   - Step 3: Provide additional information (employment, income, collateral)
   - Step 4: Upload required documents
   - Step 5: Review and submit the application
4. Accept the terms and conditions
5. Click "Submit Application" to complete the process

### Tracking Loan Application Status

To track the status of your loan application:

1. Navigate to the Loans page
2. Select "My Applications" or "Application Status"
3. The page will display all your loan applications with their current status
4. Click on an application to view detailed status information, including:
   - Application ID
   - Submission Date
   - Current Status
   - Status Timeline
   - Next Steps

### Viewing Active Loans

To view your active loans:

1. Navigate to the Loans page
2. Select "Active Loans" or "My Loans"
3. The page will display all your active loans with the following information:
   - Loan ID
   - Loan Type
   - Principal Amount
   - Outstanding Balance
   - Interest Rate
   - Monthly Payment
   - Next Payment Due Date

### Making Loan Payments

To make a loan payment:

1. Navigate to the Loans page
2. Select the loan you want to pay
3. Click "Make Payment" or "Pay Now"
4. Enter the payment amount
5. Select the payment method
6. Click "Submit" to process the payment

### Viewing Loan Payment History

To view your loan payment history:

1. Navigate to the Loans page
2. Select the loan you want to view
3. Click "Payment History" or "View Details"
4. The page will display a history of all payments made on the loan

## Document Management

### Uploading Documents

To upload documents:

1. Navigate to the Documents page or the relevant section (e.g., loan application)
2. Click "Upload Document" or "Add Document"
3. Select the document type
4. Click "Browse" or drag and drop the file
5. Wait for the upload to complete
6. Click "Submit" to save the document

### Viewing Uploaded Documents

To view your uploaded documents:

1. Navigate to the Documents page
2. The page will display all your uploaded documents with the following information:
   - Document Name
   - Document Type
   - Upload Date
   - Status (Pending, Verified, Rejected)
3. Click on a document to view or download it

### Document Requirements

Different processes require different documents. Here are the common document requirements:

- **Membership**: Valid ID, Proof of Billing, Profile Photo
- **Loan Application**: Valid ID, Proof of Income, Proof of Billing, Collateral Documents
- **Savings Account**: Valid ID, Signature Card

## Transaction History

### Viewing Transactions

To view your transaction history:

1. Click "Transactions" in the main navigation menu
2. The page will display all your transactions with the following information:
   - Date
   - Type
   - Description
   - Reference Number
   - Status
   - Amount
3. Click on a transaction to view detailed information

### Filtering Transactions

To filter your transactions:

1. Navigate to the Transactions page
2. Use the filter options at the top of the page:
   - Transaction Type (Deposit, Withdrawal, Transfer, Loan Payment, etc.)
   - Status (Completed, Pending, Failed, Cancelled)
   - Date Range
   - Amount Range
   - Search by description or reference number
3. Click "Apply Filters" to update the transaction list

### Exporting Transactions

To export your transaction history:

1. Navigate to the Transactions page
2. Apply any desired filters
3. Click "Export" or "Download"
4. Select the export format (CSV or PDF)
5. Wait for the export to generate
6. Save the file to your computer

## Damayan Feature

### Understanding Damayan

Damayan is a mutual aid program that allows members to contribute to a fund that helps members in need. The program operates on the principle of "bayanihan" or communal unity and cooperation.

### Viewing Active Funds

To view active Damayan funds:

1. Click "Damayan" in the main navigation menu or access it from the dashboard widget
2. The page will display all active funds with the following information:
   - Fund Name
   - Purpose
   - Total Contributions
   - Goal Amount
   - Beneficiary Information
   - Deadline

### Making Contributions

To contribute to a Damayan fund:

1. Navigate to the Damayan page
2. Select the fund you want to contribute to
3. Click "Contribute" or "Donate"
4. Enter the contribution amount
5. Select the payment method
6. Click "Submit" to process the contribution

### Requesting Assistance

To request assistance through Damayan:

1. Navigate to the Damayan page
2. Click "Request Assistance" or "Apply for Aid"
3. Fill out the request form with the following information:
   - Purpose of Request
   - Amount Needed
   - Supporting Documents
   - Personal Statement
4. Upload any required documents
5. Click "Submit Request" to complete the process

## Notifications

### Viewing Notifications

To view your notifications:

### Notification Settings

To manage your notification settings:

1. Navigate to your profile page
2. Click "Notification Settings"
3. Toggle on/off different types of notifications:
   - Account Updates
   - Transaction Alerts
   - Loan Status Changes
   - Document Verifications
   - Damayan Updates
4. Click "Save Settings" to apply the changes

## Security Best Practices

### Password Security

To maintain password security:

1. Use a strong, unique password for your account
2. Change your password regularly
3. Never share your password with anyone
4. Don't use the same password for multiple accounts
5. Enable two-factor authentication if available

### Account Protection

To protect your account:

1. Always log out when using public computers
2. Keep your contact information up to date
3. Review your transaction history regularly
4. Report any suspicious activity immediately
5. Be cautious of phishing attempts and only access the system through official channels
