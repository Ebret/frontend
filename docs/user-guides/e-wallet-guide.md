## Introduction

The E-Wallet feature allows you to manage your finances digitally, making it easy to send and receive money, pay bills, and make purchases. With the E-Wallet, you can:

- Deposit and withdraw funds
- Transfer money to other members
- Pay bills and make purchases
- Track your transaction history
- Set spending limits and security preferences

## Getting Started

### Creating an E-Wallet Account

To create an E-Wallet account, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to the E-Wallet registration page by clicking on "E-Wallet" in the main navigation menu and selecting "Register".
2. Fill out the registration form with your personal information:
   - First name and last name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Date of birth
3. Click "Next" to proceed to the second step.
4. Fill out the additional information:
   - Complete address
   - ID type and number (for verification)
   - Password
5. Read and accept the Data Privacy Agreement by checking the box.
6. Click "Create account" to submit your registration.
7. Check your email for a verification link and click on it to verify your account.

### Logging In

To log in to your E-Wallet account:

1. Navigate to the login page by clicking on "Login" in the main navigation menu.
2. Enter your email address and password.
3. Click "Sign in" to access your account.

### Navigating the Dashboard

After logging in, you'll be directed to your E-Wallet dashboard, which displays:

- Your current balance
- Recent transactions
- Quick action buttons for deposits, withdrawals, and transfers
- Account settings

## Managing Your E-Wallet

### Viewing Your Balance

Your current balance is displayed prominently on the dashboard. This shows the total amount of funds available in your E-Wallet.

### Viewing Transaction History

To view your transaction history:

1. Click on the "Transactions" tab in the E-Wallet dashboard.
2. Use the filters to narrow down transactions by:
   - Date range
   - Transaction type (deposit, withdrawal, transfer, payment)
   - Amount
3. Click on any transaction to view its details, including:
   - Transaction ID
   - Date and time
   - Amount
   - Type
   - Description
   - Balance after transaction

### Updating Account Settings

To update your E-Wallet settings:

1. Click on the "Settings" tab in the E-Wallet dashboard.
2. Here you can modify:
   - Daily transaction limits
   - Notification preferences
   - Security settings
3. Make your changes and click "Save" to apply them.

## Transactions

### Depositing Funds

To deposit funds into your E-Wallet:

1. Click on the "Deposit" button on the dashboard.
2. Enter the amount you want to deposit.
3. Select the deposit method:
   - Bank transfer
   - Credit/debit card
   - Cash deposit at a branch
4. Follow the instructions for your chosen method.
5. Click "Confirm" to complete the deposit.

### Withdrawing Funds

To withdraw funds from your E-Wallet:

1. Click on the "Withdraw" button on the dashboard.
2. Enter the amount you want to withdraw.
3. Select the withdrawal method:
   - Bank transfer
   - Cash pickup at a branch
   - ATM withdrawal
4. Enter the required information for your chosen method.
5. Click "Confirm" to complete the withdrawal.

### Transferring Funds

To transfer funds to another member:

1. Click on the "Transfer" button on the dashboard.
2. Enter the recipient's information:
   - E-Wallet ID or email address
   - Name (will be auto-filled if the ID or email is valid)
3. Enter the amount you want to transfer.
4. Add a description for the transfer (optional).
5. Click "Review" to verify the transfer details.
6. Click "Confirm" to complete the transfer.

### Paying Bills

To pay bills using your E-Wallet:

1. Click on the "Pay Bills" button on the dashboard.
2. Select the biller category:
   - Utilities
   - Telecommunications
   - Government
   - Loans
   - Others
3. Select the specific biller.
4. Enter your account or reference number.
5. Enter the amount to pay.
6. Click "Review" to verify the payment details.
7. Click "Confirm" to complete the payment.

## Security

### Setting Up Multi-Factor Authentication

To enhance the security of your E-Wallet, set up multi-factor authentication (MFA):

1. Go to the "Settings" tab in the E-Wallet dashboard.
2. Click on "Security".
3. Under "Multi-Factor Authentication", click "Enable".
4. Choose your preferred MFA method:
   - SMS verification
   - Email verification
   - Authenticator app
5. Follow the instructions to set up your chosen method.
6. Once set up, you'll be required to provide an additional verification code when logging in or performing sensitive transactions.

### Managing Transaction Limits

To manage your transaction limits:

1. Go to the "Settings" tab in the E-Wallet dashboard.
2. Click on "Transaction Limits".
3. Here you can set limits for:
   - Daily withdrawal limit
   - Daily transfer limit
   - Daily payment limit
4. Enter your desired limits and click "Save".

### Securing Your Account

To keep your E-Wallet account secure:

1. Use a strong, unique password.
2. Enable multi-factor authentication.
3. Never share your login credentials with anyone.
4. Be cautious of phishing attempts - the Credit Cooperative will never ask for your password via email or phone.
5. Regularly check your transaction history for unauthorized transactions.
6. Log out when you're done using the E-Wallet, especially on shared devices.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### I forgot my password

1. Click on "Forgot password?" on the login page.
2. Enter your email address.
3. Check your email for a password reset link.
4. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password.

#### My transaction failed

1. Check your balance to ensure you have sufficient funds.
2. Verify that you've entered the correct recipient information.
3. Check if you've reached your daily transaction limit.
4. Try again after a few minutes.
5. If the issue persists, contact customer support.

#### I can't log in

1. Ensure you're using the correct email address and password.
2. Check if Caps Lock is turned on.
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies.
4. Try using a different browser or device.
5. If you still can't log in, click on "Forgot password?" to reset your password.

## FAQ

### General Questions

#### What is an E-Wallet?

An E-Wallet is a digital wallet that allows you to store, send, and receive money electronically. It provides a convenient and secure way to manage your finances without the need for physical cash.

#### Is my money safe in the E-Wallet?

Yes, your money is safe in the E-Wallet. We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption and multi-factor authentication, to protect your account. Additionally, your funds are insured up to a certain amount.

#### Are there any fees for using the E-Wallet?

Most basic transactions, such as deposits, withdrawals, and transfers to other members, are free. However, there may be fees for certain services, such as expedited withdrawals or bill payments to certain billers. You can view our fee schedule in the "Fees" section of the E-Wallet dashboard.

### Transactions

#### How long do transactions take to process?

- Deposits: Depending on the method, deposits can be instant (credit/debit card) or take 1-3 business days (bank transfer).
- Withdrawals: Withdrawals to bank accounts typically take 1-3 business days. Cash pickups are usually available within 24 hours.
- Transfers: Transfers to other members are processed instantly.
- Bill payments: Bill payments are typically processed within 24 hours, but it may take the biller 2-3 business days to reflect the payment in their system.

#### What is the maximum amount I can transfer?

The default maximum transfer amount is ₱50,000 per day. However, you can adjust this limit in your account settings, subject to verification requirements.

#### Can I cancel a transaction?

You can cancel a transaction only if it has not been processed yet. To cancel a pending transaction:
1. Go to the "Transactions" tab.
2. Find the pending transaction.
3. Click on "Cancel" next to the transaction.
4. Confirm the cancellation.

### Account Management

#### How do I update my personal information?

To update your personal information:
1. Go to the "Profile" tab in the E-Wallet dashboard.
2. Click on "Edit" next to the information you want to update.
3. Make your changes and click "Save".

Note that some changes, such as your name or ID information, may require additional verification.

#### What should I do if I suspect unauthorized activity on my account?

If you suspect unauthorized activity:
1. Change your password immediately.
2. Enable multi-factor authentication if you haven't already.
3. Contact customer support to report the suspicious activity.
4. Review your transaction history and report any unauthorized transactions.

#### Can I have multiple E-Wallets?

No, each member is limited to one E-Wallet account. This helps us maintain security and comply with regulatory requirements.
