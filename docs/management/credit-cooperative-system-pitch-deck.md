## System Overview

The Credit Cooperative System is a modular, white-label solution designed specifically for credit cooperatives in the Philippines, featuring:

- **Core Banking Platform**: Complete member and account management
- **E-Wallet Integration**: Seamless digital transactions
- **Damayan Feature**: Community-based mutual aid system
- **Role-Based Access**: Tailored interfaces for all stakeholders
- **Multi-Environment Architecture**: Ensuring stability and quality

---

## Key Differentiators

- **API-First Development**: Enabling seamless integration with third-party services
- **Philippine-Focused**: Designed with ₱ currency symbol and local compliance in mind
- **Comprehensive Role Support**: From Board of Directors to Tellers to Members
- **Real-Time Features**: Instant notifications and dashboard updates
- **Enterprise-Grade Security**: Bank-level data protection and compliance
