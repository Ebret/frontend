# Member Assets and Liabilities Form (Template)

This template captures a member’s financial capacity for credit assessment and compliance.

## Member Information
- member_id
- full_name
- member_no
- contact_number
- address

## Assets
### Cash and Bank
- cash_on_hand
- bank_accounts (bank_name, account_type, balance)

### Real Property
- property_address
- property_type (residential/commercial/agri)
- ownership (owned/leased)
- estimated_value

### Vehicles
- vehicle_type
- year_model
- estimated_value

### Business Assets
- business_name
- asset_description
- estimated_value

## Liabilities
### Loans
- lender
- loan_type
- original_amount
- outstanding_balance
- monthly_payment

### Credit Cards
- issuer
- credit_limit
- current_balance

### Other Obligations
- obligation_description
- outstanding_balance
- monthly_payment

## Income
- primary_income_source
- monthly_income
- other_income_sources
- total_monthly_income

## Declarations
- declaration_statement
- member_signature
- signed_date
- reviewed_by
- reviewed_at
