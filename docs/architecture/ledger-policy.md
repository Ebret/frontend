# Ledger Policy

## Core Principles
- No direct edits to posted financial records.
- Corrections are made via reversals and new entries.
- All postings are tied to source documents and approvals.

## Back-Dated Postings
- Allowed only within an open period.
- Must include justification and approver.
- System records both effective date and posting date.

## Adjustments and Reversals
- Reversals reference the original entry id.
- Adjustments require a new entry with clear reason codes.
- Reversal entries inherit original GL and SL references.

## Audit and Approval
- All adjustments require maker-checker.
- Audit log includes before and after values, actor, role, and timestamp.
