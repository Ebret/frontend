# Glossary

## Abbreviations
- CDA: Cooperative Development Authority
- CAPR: Cooperative Annual Performance Report
- CTE: Cooperative Tax Exemption evidence pack
- BIR: Bureau of Internal Revenue
- COA: Chart of Accounts
- GL: General Ledger
- SL: Subsidiary Ledger
- KYC: Know Your Customer
- SoD: Segregation of Duties

## Terms
- Maker-checker: Control pattern where one user creates a transaction and another approves it.
- Posting batch: Group of journal entries posted together as one accounting action.
- Subledger: Detailed ledger that rolls up into the GL.
