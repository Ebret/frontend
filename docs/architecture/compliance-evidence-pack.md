# Compliance Evidence Pack Template

## CDA Reporting
- Cooperative profile snapshot
- Governance resolution pack
- CAPR dataset export with validation report
- Chart of accounts mapping version
- Accountable officer sign-off

## BIR Tax-Exemption Support
- Member TIN completeness report
- Tax exemption certificates and evidence
- Submission acknowledgement and reference numbers

## Member KYC and Financial Capacity Evidence
- Government ID set (front/back), validity dates
- Proof of address (electric, water, internet bill, lease/rent agreement)
- Proof of income or employment (payslips, COE, ITR if available)
- Assets and liabilities declaration (self-declared form)
- Source of funds declaration
- Document review logs with reviewer and timestamp

## HR/Payroll Compliance Evidence
- SSS Employer Registration reference and PRN remittance receipts
- Pag-IBIG Employer Data Form (HQP-PFF-002) and acknowledgement
- PhilHealth ER2/RF-1 reporting logs and EPRS remittance reports
- Payroll register with employee contributions and employer share

## Evidence Metadata
- report_period
- generated_at
- generated_by
- dataset_version
- parameters
- attachments
