# Reporting Calendars and Reminder Rules

## CDA (CAPR)
- Frequency: Annual
- Due: Within 120 days after calendar year end
- Reminders: T-60, T-30, T-10, T-3 days
- Escalation: Board Secretary if T-10 not submitted

## BIR (CTE and Withholding)
- CTE Renewal: At least 2 months before expiration
- Withholding: Follow current BIR schedule per RDO
- Reminders: T-45, T-20, T-7 days for CTE renewal
- Escalation: Compliance Officer if T-7 not submitted

## SSS
- Employee Reporting: Within 30 days of hiring
- Remittance: Per prescribed schedule
- Reminders: T-5, T-2 days before remittance due
- Escalation: Accounting Officer if T-2 not submitted

## Pag-IBIG
- Employer Registration: One-time, as required
- Remittance: Per prescribed schedule
- Reminders: T-5, T-2 days before remittance due
- Escalation: Accounting Officer if T-2 not submitted

## PhilHealth
- ER2/RF-1 Reporting: Within 30 days of hire/separation
- Remittance: PEN-based schedule
- Reminders: T-5, T-2 days before remittance due
- Escalation: Compliance Officer if T-2 not submitted
