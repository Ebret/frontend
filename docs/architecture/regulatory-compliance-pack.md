# Regulatory Compliance Pack (Philippines)

This pack summarizes compliance touchpoints for cooperatives with CDA, BIR, SSS, Pag-IBIG, and PhilHealth. It provides system fields, evidence, and workflows. Final interpretations should be validated with counsel, CPA, and compliance officers.

## 1. CDA (Cooperative Development Authority)

### CAPR / CAPRIS (Annual Reporting)
**Key Requirements**
- CAPR submission is annual and filed via CDAĺs CAPRIS online system. ?cite?turn1search0?
- CAPR requires complete fields; CDA guidance indicates no field should be left unfilled. ?cite?turn1search0?
- CAPR submission deadline: within 120 days after calendar year end. ?cite?turn1search0?
- A signed CAPR copy must be submitted to the Extension Office within five (5) days from electronic submission. ?cite?turn1search0?

**System Fields**
- Cooperative registration identifiers (CIN, registration number, type, category)
- Branch and region
- Accountable officer name, role, signature metadata
- Report period and submission timestamp

**Evidence Pack**
- CAPR dataset extract + validation checks
- CAPR submission acknowledgement and reference ID
- Signed CAPR copy metadata

## 2. BIR (Bureau of Internal Revenue)

### Cooperative Tax Exemption (CTE)
**Key Requirements**
- Applications for CTE use BIR Form 1945 and require CDA registration documents, Certificate of Good Standing/Compliance, BIR registration, and a certified list of members with TIN and capital contributions. ?cite?turn1search1?
- Renewal of the CTE requires filing BIR Form 1945 at least two (2) months before expiration. ?cite?turn1search2?

**System Fields**
- CDA registration number and certificate details
- BIR Certificate of Registration reference
- Member list with TIN and capital contribution
- CTE application status and dates

**Evidence Pack**
- BIR Form 1945 packet and attachments
- Member TIN list certification metadata

### Withholding on Compensation (Employer Compliance)
**Key Requirements**
- Employer withholding obligations apply; exact forms and schedules must follow current BIR guidance and the cooperativeĺs RDO. ?cite?turn1search3?

**System Fields**
- Employee compensation data by period
- Withholding tax computation and remittance reference
- Filing period and payment date

**Evidence Pack**
- Filed withholding return references and payment confirmation

## 3. SSS (Social Security System)

### Employer Registration and Reporting
**Key Requirements**
- Employers must register and secure an Employer ID, report employees for coverage within 30 days of hiring, and remit contributions using a PRN within the prescribed schedule. ?cite?turn1search4?
- Employers must maintain true and accurate employment and payroll records. ?cite?turn1search4?

**System Fields**
- Employer ID, company email, contact info
- Employee SS number, TIN, employment date
- Contribution schedule and remittance reference

**Evidence Pack**
- Employer registration reference
- Employee reporting logs and PRN receipts

## 4. Pag-IBIG Fund (HDMF)

### Employer Registration
**Key Requirements**
- Employer registration requires the Employerĺs Data Form (HQP-PFF-002) and supporting documents per the checklist in the form. ?cite?turn1search5?

**System Fields**
- Employer ID and registration date
- Employer data form reference
- Proof of business existence document reference

**Evidence Pack**
- Employer registration acknowledgment
- Data form and supporting documents

## 5. PhilHealth

### Employer Reporting and Remittance
**Key Requirements**
- Employers must submit ER2 for newly hired employees and RF-1 for separated employees within 30 days. ?cite?turn1search6?
- Premium payments and remittance reporting are done via EPRS, with due dates based on the employerĺs PEN schedule. ?cite?turn1search7?

**System Fields**
- Employer PEN and EPRS account reference
- Employee PhilHealth number
- Remittance schedule and payment confirmation

**Evidence Pack**
- EPRS remittance report and payment confirmation

## 6. Member KYC and Proof-of-Address/Income

**Document Types to Capture**
- Valid government IDs
- Proof of address (electric, water, internet bills; lease/rent agreement)
- Proof of income or employment (payslips, COE)
- Assets and liabilities declaration

**System Fields**
- Document type, issue date, expiry date
- Source and reference ID
- Review status and reviewer

**Evidence Pack**
- Document checklist completion
- Review logs and approvals

## 7. Governance (BOD and Officers)

**Required Records**
- Board resolutions and committee decisions
- Delegated approval matrix
- Accountable officer certifications

**System Fields**
- Resolution number and date
- Approver identity and role
- Decision rationale and attachments

**Evidence Pack**
- Signed resolution documents
- Approval logs and audit trail
